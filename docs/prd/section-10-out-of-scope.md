# Section 10 - Out-of-Scope and Assumptions

## 10.1 Out-of-Scope
- UI or dashboards
- Human players
- Persistent authentication across leagues
- Distributed deployment across multiple machines
- Network security beyond localhost assumptions

## 10.2 Assumptions
- Agents are intended to follow protocol and operate on localhost
- Time synchronization is reliable for UTC timestamps
- The system can read/write persisted artifacts and logs locally
